SMKN 1 Lemahsugih

SMK Negeri 1 Lemahsugih is a vocational school located in a mountainous area, precisely in the east of the Majalengka region, namely in Lemahsugih District. Although located in a mountainous area, SMKN 1 Lemahsugih is a large school, has many students, and can compete in formal and non-formal education with other schools.
